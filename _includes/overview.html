Overview: Flightpath to Intelligence

AirStar

UAV multifunctional system

AirStar integrates an LLM as its cognitive core, enabling intuitive voice/gesture control, geospatial navigation, and advanced features like automated filming, paving the way for next-gen UAV assistants.

UAV_Flow
AeroDuo

Navigation

This stage addresses UAV navigation. UAV_Flow targets short-range, fine-grained navigation, while AeroDuo focuses on long-range planning, enabling UAVs to navigate complex environments effectively at different scales.

Video2BEV

Perception and Geo-localization

We redefined drone geo-localization by converting videos into detailed Bird's Eye View (BEV) maps using Gaussian Splatting-based 3D reconstruction.

OpenUAV

UAV Simulation Platform

We built a realistic simulation platform and the UAV-Need-Help benchmark to support vision-language navigation.
