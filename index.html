Overview

We specialize in large model-enabled intelligent human-drone interaction research, achieving breakthroughs in language-guided UAV control—including short-range operation, long-range planning, and multi-agent collaboration—with integrated multi-modal interaction systems.

Future efforts will focus on cutting-edge directions: control-planning dual systems, edge-device lightweight large model deployment, heterogeneous intelligent agent coordination, and drone-manipulator hybrid systems.
